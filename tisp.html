TISP Week is a Region 8 program aimed at promoting the educational advantages of engineering by fostering collaboration between teachers and students through engaging and innovative lesson plans. During this week, TISP Workshops are held across Europe, the Middle East, and Africa, organized by the participating Sections. Each workshop provides training to a minimum of 30 school teachers, with IEEE-trained volunteers delivering the sessions and implementing lesson plans from TryEngineering.org
